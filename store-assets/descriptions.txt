Night White — warm glow, soothing sounds, sleep better

## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Night White turns your phone into a gentle nightlight and sleep aid. Perfect for bedtime, meditation, or creating a calm atmosphere.

**Features:**

• **Warm Glow** — Adjustable color temperature from deep amber to soft white, with smooth brightness control
• **Soothing Sounds** — Choose from white, pink, or brown noise — or drift off to built-in lullabies like Twinkle Twinkle and Brahms' Lullaby
• **Sleep Timer** — Set the light and audio to turn off automatically after 1, 2, 4, or 8 hours
• **Flip to Dim** — Place your phone face-down to automatically dim the screen without touching it
• **Quick Settings Tile** — Toggle your nightlight instantly from the notification shade
• **Battery Protection** — Automatically turns off when battery drops below 15%
• **Zero Distractions** — Controls hide after 20 seconds so the screen stays clean and dark

**Perfect for:**
→ Parents soothing babies to sleep
→ Adults who need darkness but want a gentle glow
→ Meditation and relaxation sessions
→ Anyone who struggles with screen light at night

**Privacy:** Night White works entirely offline. No data collection, no ads, no internet permission required.
