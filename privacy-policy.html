Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Overview

Night White is a nightlight and sleep aid application. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data. In short: we don't collect any personal data.

Data Collection

Night White does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage data or analytics are gathered
  • No device identifiers are accessed
  • No location data is collected
  • No contacts, photos, or files are accessed

Local Storage

Your app preferences (such as brightness level, color temperature, and sound settings) are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not shared with any third party
  • Is automatically removed when you uninstall the app

Permissions

Night White requests the following permissions, all of which are used solely for app functionality:

  • Foreground Service — Keeps the nightlight and audio running while the app is in use
  • Modify Audio Settings — Controls audio playback for noise and lullaby features
  • Post Notifications — Displays a notification while the nightlight is active (required by Android for foreground services)

No permissions are used for data collection or tracking.

Third-Party Services

Night White does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app works entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Children's Privacy

Night White does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Google Play Store listing for Night White.
